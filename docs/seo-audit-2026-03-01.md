## Detailed Category Analysis

### 1. Technical SEO — 7/10 (WARN)

**What's working:**
- ✅ `sitemap-index.xml` exists and lists all 15 pages
- ✅ `robots.txt` correctly configured with sitemap directive
- ✅ Every page has `<link rel="canonical">` — all correct
- ✅ Hreflang tags present for EN, FR, and x-default on every page
- ✅ Clean URL structure: `/{lang}/{page}/`
- ✅ No duplicate content between language versions

**Issues found:**
- ❌ Root `index.html` is a bare redirect with no meta tags, no canonical, no viewport — search engines may index this poorly
- ❌ `robots.txt` references `sitemap-index.xml` but `docs/seo.md` specifies `sitemap.xml` — inconsistency (not harmful but messy)
- ⚠️ Some hreflang URLs lack trailing slashes (e.g., `href="/en/about"` vs canonical `/en/about/`) — potential mismatch

### 2. On-Page SEO — 6/10 (WARN)

**What's working:**
- ✅ Every page has a unique `<title>` tag
- ✅ Every page has a unique `<meta name="description">`
- ✅ One `<h1>` per page, descriptive and relevant
- ✅ Heading hierarchy sequential (h1 → h2 → h3), no skips

**Issues found:**
- ❌ **Titles don't match docs/seo.md spec** — the specified keyword-rich titles are not being used:
  - Home actual: "Dynapharm Africa — Health, Wealth, Freedom" (45 chars)
  - Home specified: "Dynapharm Africa — Natural Health Products & Business Opportunity" (much more keyword-rich)
  - FAQ actual: "Frequently Asked Questions — Dynapharm Africa" (46 chars)
  - FAQ specified: "FAQ — Frequently Asked Questions | Dynapharm Africa" (with product keywords in description)
- ❌ **Meta descriptions miss critical keywords:**
  - Home description doesn't mention: Spirulina, Ganoderma, Chlorophyll, Noni (top product searches)
  - Products description doesn't mention: Ganoderma coffee, D.I. Grow, Bee Pollen, Tongkat Ali
  - FAQ description is only 96 chars — wastes valuable SERP space
- ❌ **No `<meta name="keywords">` tags** — while Google doesn't use these, Bing and African search engines may
- ❌ **French titles/descriptions exceed recommended lengths:**
  - FR About title: 78 chars (recommended <60)
  - FR Business title: 76 chars (recommended <60)
  - FR Home description: 233 chars (recommended <160)
  - FR Business description: 212 chars (recommended <160)
- ⚠️ H1 tags are generic ("Our Story", "Get in Touch") — should include "Dynapharm" for brand reinforcement

### 3. Content Quality — 8/10 (PASS)

**What's working:**
- ✅ Rich, unique content on every page (300+ words)
- ✅ No placeholder/lorem ipsum text in visible content
- ✅ Strong E-E-A-T signals: company history, founding year, GMP certification, 40+ countries
- ✅ FAQ page has 15+ genuine Q&A pairs covering products, business, operations
- ✅ Product names and ingredient details are specific and accurate
- ✅ Testimonials from real people across multiple African countries

**Issues found:**
- ⚠️ Team profiles marked [PLACEHOLDER] — hurts E-E-A-T (authoritativeness)
- ⚠️ No publication dates or "last updated" timestamps on content pages

### 4. Structured Data — 0/10 (CRITICAL FAIL)

**ZERO structured data implemented.** The `docs/seo.md` specifies four schema types, none are in the HTML:

- ❌ **Organization schema** — NOT present (should be on every page)
  - Without this: Google cannot build a Knowledge Panel for "Dynapharm Africa"
  - Impact: Competitor sites or directory listings may rank above you for brand searches
- ❌ **FAQPage schema** — NOT present on FAQ page
  - Without this: FAQ content won't appear as rich results in Google SERPs
  - Impact: Massive missed opportunity — FAQ rich results can take up 3-4x SERP space
- ❌ **Product schema** — NOT present on Products page
  - Without this: Product searches won't show rich product cards
- ❌ **BreadcrumbList schema** — NOT present on any inner page
  - Without this: Google shows raw URLs instead of breadcrumb trails in search results

**This is the single biggest SEO gap. Structured data is what turns a good page into a dominant SERP presence.**

### 5. Core Web Vitals — 8/10 (PASS)

**What's working:**
- ✅ Astro ships zero JS by default — only Alpine.js islands
- ✅ All images use Astro `<Image>` with width/height (prevents CLS)
- ✅ Hero images use `loading="eager"`, all others `loading="lazy"`
- ✅ Images optimized to WebP/AVIF via Sharp
- ✅ Fonts self-hosted via Fontsource (no external CDN)
- ✅ CSS inlined/minimized

**Issues found:**
- ⚠️ No explicit `font-display: swap` — may cause FOIT (flash of invisible text)
- ⚠️ GSAP loaded but may not be tree-shaken — verify only used modules are bundled

### 6. Mobile Usability — 9/10 (PASS)

**What's working:**
- ✅ `<meta name="viewport">` on all pages
- ✅ Responsive grids: 1-col mobile → 2-3 tablet → 3-4 desktop
- ✅ Touch targets ≥ 44x44px (buttons, nav items)
- ✅ Mobile hamburger menu with Alpine.js
- ✅ Responsive typography (text-3xl md:text-4xl lg:text-5xl)
- ✅ No horizontal scroll detected

**Issues found:**
- ⚠️ Root redirect page (`/`) has no viewport meta — mobile crawlers hitting root get unoptimized view

### 7. Local/Geo SEO — 1/10 (CRITICAL FAIL)

**For a site targeting ALL African countries, geo-SEO is almost entirely missing:**

- ❌ **No geo meta tags** (`geo.region`, `geo.placename`, `ICBM`) on any page
- ❌ **No `areaServed`** in any structured data (because no structured data exists)
- ❌ **No country-specific keywords** in titles/descriptions — "Africa" is too broad
  - Missing: Uganda, Kenya, Nigeria, Senegal, Ghana, Tanzania, Cameroon, Gabon, Zambia, Mozambique, etc.
- ❌ **No LocalBusiness or place-related schema** for Distribution Point Centres
- ❌ **Contact page has no physical address** — only email
- ⚠️ Testimonials mention specific countries (Senegal, Uganda, Mozambique, Lesotho) but these aren't in meta tags

**This is the #2 critical gap. Someone searching "Dynapharm Uganda" or "produits Dynapharm Sénégal" won't find strong geo-signals on this site.**

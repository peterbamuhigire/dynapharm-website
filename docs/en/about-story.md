## Section: Origins

### Heading: Rooted in Nature, Built for the World

In 1981, in the vibrant city of Penang, Malaysia, Mr. Oi Ho Chooi founded Dynapharm with a bold vision: to improve the quality of life for people everywhere through the finest natural health products. Rooted in centuries of traditional Asian herbal wisdom — Ganoderma (Lingzhi mushroom), Tongkat Ali, Spirulina, Chlorophyll — Mr. Oi set out to make these time-honoured ingredients accessible, affordable, and convenient.

From the beginning, Dynapharm's approach combined the best of tradition and modernity. Traditional ingredients valued for centuries in Chinese, Malay, and Southeast Asian wellness practices were formulated into modern product formats — tablets, capsules, powders, beverages, and personal care products. In 1990, the company moved to its current GMP-certified manufacturing premises, cementing its commitment to international quality standards.

In 1995, Dynapharm entered the world of person-to-person selling through network marketing, opening the door for individuals everywhere to share its products and build their own businesses. What started as one man's dream in Penang grew into an international wellness company. Today, Dynapharm's product catalogue spans over 90 items across eight categories, reaching communities in more than 40 countries.

## Section: The Africa Chapter

### Heading: Empowering Africa Through Health, Wealth & Freedom

In 2005, through the vision and determination of Ms Esther Ampumuza, Dynapharm arrived in Africa — marking a defining chapter in the company's story. Africa — a continent rich in natural healing traditions and entrepreneurial energy — proved to be a natural fit for Dynapharm's mission.

Ms Ampumuza recognised that African communities have long valued natural remedies, herbal preparations, and holistic approaches to wellbeing. Dynapharm's products, rooted in similar philosophies from across Asia, resonated deeply with this heritage. Spirulina, Ganoderma, Bee Pollen, Noni — these ingredients, though originating from different continents, spoke the same language of natural wellness.

But Dynapharm brought more than products. Through its network marketing model, the company offered something equally valuable: a genuine business opportunity. In markets where formal employment can be scarce and entrepreneurial spirit runs deep, Dynapharm gave individuals a path to financial independence — requiring no formal qualifications, no large investment, and no previous business experience.

Today, Dynapharm Africa operates across the continent through independent franchises and a growing network of Distribution Point Centres (DPCs) — local wellness hubs that serve their communities with products, training, and support.

## Section: The Model

### Heading: Building Entrepreneurs, Not Just Selling Products

Dynapharm's network marketing model is designed around a simple principle: when people succeed, the company succeeds. Every distributor is a partner in the Dynapharm mission — not merely a salesperson, but an entrepreneur building their own business.

The journey begins with the Distributor Registration Kit. From day one, new distributors receive comprehensive training — product knowledge, sales techniques, network building strategies, and leadership development. Mentorship from upline leaders provides guidance and accountability.

The rank progression — from Distributor (DI) through Manager (M), Senior Manager (SM), Ruby Manager (RM), Pearl Manager (PM), Diamond Manager (DM), to Crown Diamond Manager (CDM) — marks a journey of personal and professional growth. Each rank represents not just higher Business Volume (BV) thresholds, but deeper commitment, larger teams, and greater impact.

Income comes through multiple streams: personal sales margins, team performance bonuses, and rank-based rewards. Success is not instant — it requires effort, consistency, and genuine care for customers and team members. But for those who commit, Dynapharm offers a proven path to building something meaningful.

Every product is manufactured to international quality standards, using carefully selected natural ingredients from trusted sources worldwide.

## Section: Our Values

### Heading: What We Stand For

**Quality First**: Every product in the Dynapharm catalogue meets international manufacturing standards. Quality is not negotiable — from ingredient sourcing to final packaging, every step is controlled and verified.

**Natural Always**: Our products are rooted in nature. Spirulina from nutrient-rich waters, Ganoderma cultivated with care, herbs sourced from traditional growing regions. We combine nature's ingredients with modern science to create products that are both effective and safe.

**People Before Profit**: Our distributors are not salespeople — they are partners. Their success is our success. We invest in training, mentorship, and support because we believe that empowered people build better businesses and stronger communities.

**Community Impact**: Healthy people build healthy communities. Every product sold, every distributor trained, every DPC opened contributes to a larger mission: making natural health and financial opportunity accessible to all.

**Integrity**: We present our business honestly. Our products are food supplements, not medicines. Our business opportunity requires genuine effort and commitment. We do not promise overnight success — we promise support, training, and a proven model for those willing to do the work.

## Role: Founder

**Name**: Mr. Oi Ho Chooi

**Title**: Founder, Dynapharm International

**Bio**: In 1981, in Penang, Malaysia, Mr. Oi Ho Chooi founded Dynapharm with a single, powerful vision: to improve the quality of life for people through natural health products. With determination and unwavering commitment to quality, he built Dynapharm from a small manufacturing operation into an international wellness company. Under his leadership, the company moved to a GMP-certified manufacturing facility in 1990 and entered the world of network marketing in 1995. Today, his vision has reached over 40 countries, empowering communities across Africa and beyond with premium natural health products and genuine business opportunities.

## Role: Africa Pioneer

**Name**: Ms Esther Ampumuza

**Title**: Pioneer & Director, Dynapharm Africa

**Bio**: Ms Esther Ampumuza is the visionary who brought Dynapharm to the African continent in 2005. Recognising the natural alignment between Dynapharm's mission and Africa's rich traditions of natural wellness, Ms Ampumuza worked tirelessly to establish the first African franchise. Her leadership and entrepreneurial spirit transformed Dynapharm from an Asian company into a truly global enterprise, with Africa becoming the company's strongest growth market. Through her efforts, Dynapharm now operates across 40+ African countries, serving communities through a network of Distribution Point Centres and empowering thousands of distributors.

**Photo**: ESTHER-AMPUMUZA.jpg

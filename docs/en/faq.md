## About Dynapharm

### What is Dynapharm?

Dynapharm is an international health and wellness company that manufactures and distributes natural health products through a network marketing (MLM) model. Founded in Malaysia, Dynapharm has grown to operate in over 40 countries across Africa and beyond. Our product range includes over 90 items spanning health beverages, nutritional supplements, herbal formulations, personal care, wellness foods, and agricultural solutions.

### Where does Dynapharm operate?

Dynapharm operates in over 40 countries worldwide, with a strong presence across the African continent. Each country is served through an independent franchise supported by local Distribution Point Centres (DPCs). For enquiries about product availability in your area, please contact us at info@dynapharmafrica.com.

### How long has Dynapharm been in business?

Dynapharm was founded in 1981 in Penang, Malaysia by Mr. Oi Ho Chooi. The company has over four decades of experience in natural health products. In 1990, Dynapharm moved to its current GMP-certified manufacturing facility. In 1995, the company entered network marketing, and in 2005, Ms Esther Ampumuza brought Dynapharm to Africa — establishing what would become the company's strongest growth region, now spanning 40+ countries.

### Are Dynapharm products safe?

Dynapharm products are manufactured to international quality standards in our GMP-certified facility, using carefully selected natural ingredients. Our products are food supplements and personal care items — they are not medicines. As with any supplement, we recommend consulting a qualified healthcare professional before starting a new product, particularly if you have existing health conditions, are pregnant, or are taking medication.

### What makes Dynapharm different from other health supplement companies?

Dynapharm combines the best of traditional herbal wisdom with modern manufacturing standards. Our products feature ingredients valued for centuries in Asian and global wellness traditions — Ganoderma (Lingzhi mushroom), Spirulina, Tongkat Ali, Bee Pollen, and Chlorophyll, among others. Beyond products, Dynapharm offers a genuine business opportunity through its network marketing model, enabling individuals to build sustainable businesses whilst promoting wellness in their communities. Our local Distribution Point Centres (DPCs) provide hands-on support that many supplement companies lack.

### What is Ganoderma and why is it in so many Dynapharm products?

Ganoderma, also known as Lingzhi (Chinese) or Reishi (Japanese), is a large, dark mushroom with a woody texture that has been revered in traditional Asian medicine for over 2,000 years. Known as the "mushroom of immortality," it was historically reserved for royalty in ancient China. Ganoderma is rich in bioactive compounds including beta-glucan polysaccharides and triterpenes, and is traditionally valued as an adaptogen. Dynapharm incorporates Ganoderma into coffees, capsules, soaps, toothpaste, shower gel, and lotion — making it easy to incorporate this traditional ingredient into your daily routine.

### What is Spirulina?

Spirulina is a blue-green microalgae that has been consumed for centuries — by the ancient Aztecs in Mexico and the Kanembu people near Lake Chad in Africa. It is exceptionally nutrient-dense, containing 60-70% protein by weight along with all essential amino acids, iron, calcium, magnesium, B-vitamins, and vitamin E. NASA has studied Spirulina as a potential food for astronauts. Dynapharm offers Spirulina in tablet form and as a sea water powder.

### What is D.I. Grow and how does it work?

D.I. Grow is Dynapharm's liquid bio-fertiliser, available in two formulations: **Red** (for flowering and fruiting plants) and **Green** (for leafy growth and vegetative development). It is a natural, plant-friendly alternative to conventional chemical fertilisers. D.I. Grow is diluted with water and applied to crops, gardens, and potted plants. It is available in 250ML, 1 litre, and 4 litre sizes.

### What is Dyna Tonic?

Dyna Tonic is a herbal tonic formulated from 15 traditional herbs rooted in Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) principles. The formula includes Radix Codonopsis (Dang Shen), Astragalus (Huang Qi), Poria (Fu Ling), Liquorice root (Gan Cao), Fo-Ti (He Shou Wu), Cistanche, Chinese Yam, Eucommia bark, Sour Jujube seed, and other traditional herbs. It is a comprehensive herbal blend that combines Qi-tonifying, blood-nourishing, and calming herbs. Available in 150ML and 780ML sizes.

## Business Opportunity

### How do I become a Dynapharm distributor?

Becoming a Dynapharm distributor is straightforward. You register through an existing distributor (your sponsor/upline) and receive the Distributor Registration Kit. From there, you receive training in product knowledge, sales techniques, and network building. No formal qualifications or prior business experience are required. To express your interest, contact us at info@dynapharmafrica.com or speak with an existing distributor.

### What is the Distributor Registration Kit?

The Distributor Registration Kit is your entry point into the Dynapharm business. It provides everything a new distributor needs to get started, including registration materials and initial product samples. The kit represents a low-barrier entry point — no large upfront investment is required.

### How does the network marketing model work at Dynapharm?

Dynapharm uses a network marketing (MLM) distribution model. As a distributor, you earn through two primary channels: **personal sales** (selling products directly to customers) and **team building** (recruiting and supporting other distributors in your network). Your network can extend up to 20 generations deep. Each person you directly recruit forms a "leg" in your network, and that leg includes their entire sub-network. Your success depends on both your personal effort and your ability to build, train, and support a productive team.

### What are the ranks and how do I advance?

Dynapharm has seven achievement ranks, each requiring a minimum Business Volume (BV) threshold:

| Rank | Code | Minimum BV |
|------|------|-----------|
| Distributor | DI | 0 |
| Manager | M | 500 |
| Senior Manager | SM | 1,000 |
| Ruby Manager | RM | 2,500 |
| Pearl Manager | PM | 5,000 |
| Diamond Manager | DM | 10,000 |
| Crown Diamond Manager | CDM | 20,000 |

Advancement is based on your accumulated Business Volume (BV). Higher ranks bring greater recognition, responsibilities, and earning potential. Progression requires sustained effort, team building, and consistent product sales.

### What is Business Volume (BV) and why does it matter?

Business Volume (BV) is the metric used to measure your sales performance and determine your rank within Dynapharm. Each product carries a BV value. When a sale is completed and fully paid, the BV is credited to your account. Your accumulated BV determines your rank qualification and contributes to bonus calculations. BV is recognised on the date of full payment — ensuring that only completed transactions count towards your performance.

### How do I earn with Dynapharm?

Dynapharm distributors earn through multiple streams:

- **Personal sales margin** — the difference between the distributor price and the retail price when selling directly to customers
- **Team performance bonuses** — bonuses based on the sales performance of your network (downline distributors)
- **Rank-based rewards** — additional benefits and recognition as you advance through the seven ranks

Bonuses are calculated by Dynapharm headquarters and distributed through the franchise system. Your earnings depend directly on your personal effort, your team's performance, and your commitment to the business. There are no guaranteed income levels — success requires consistent work.

### Do I need experience to start?

No prior business or sales experience is required. Dynapharm provides comprehensive training for all new distributors, covering product knowledge, sales techniques, network building strategies, and leadership development. Your sponsor (upline) provides mentorship and guidance from day one, and your local DPC offers ongoing training and support.

### What training and support is provided?

Every Dynapharm distributor receives:

- **Product knowledge training** — understanding every product, its ingredients, and how to recommend it
- **Sales techniques** — ethical, effective approaches to sharing products
- **Network building** — strategies for growing and managing your team
- **Leadership development** — for Managers and above, training in mentorship and team leadership
- **Ongoing support** — from your upline, your DPC, and the broader Dynapharm community
- **Events and conferences** — regular gatherings for training, networking, and recognition

Training is available both online and in-person at Distribution Point Centres.

### What is a Distribution Point Centre (DPC)?

A Distribution Point Centre (DPC) is a local Dynapharm hub — a physical location where distributors can access products, receive training, and connect with the Dynapharm community. DPCs are operated within each franchise country and serve as the foundation of Dynapharm's "Global Reach, Local Presence" approach. Some DPCs are company-operated, whilst others are managed by experienced distributors.

### Is this a pyramid scheme?

No. Dynapharm is a legitimate network marketing company with real, physical products that customers purchase and use. Income is generated from actual product sales, not merely from recruiting new members. The key distinction: in a pyramid scheme, money flows primarily from recruitment fees with no real product. In Dynapharm's model, distributors earn from selling genuine health, personal care, and agricultural products to real customers. Success requires effort, product knowledge, and genuine customer relationships.

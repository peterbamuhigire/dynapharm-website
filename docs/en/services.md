## Service: Business Opportunity

**Tagline**: Build your own wellness business — your way, at your pace

Start your own health and wellness business with a low initial investment. Dynapharm's network marketing model offers a clear path from Distributor to Crown Diamond Manager, with training, mentorship, and support at every step.

- **Low barrier to entry**: Register as a distributor with a Distributor Registration Kit — no large upfront investment
- **No experience required**: Comprehensive training in product knowledge, sales, and team building
- **7 achievement ranks**: Distributor (DI), Manager (M), Senior Manager (SM), Ruby Manager (RM), Pearl Manager (PM), Diamond Manager (DM), Crown Diamond Manager (CDM)
- **Multiple income streams**: Personal sales margin, team performance bonuses, and rank-based rewards
- **Flexible schedule**: Build your business alongside your existing commitments — part-time or full-time
- **Proven model**: Thousands of active distributors across 40+ countries have built successful businesses with Dynapharm
